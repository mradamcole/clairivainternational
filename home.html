A core strength of Brad's leadership lies in integrating Quality Assurance (QA) and Regulatory Affairs (RA) into operational strategy. His ability to bridge technical regulatory requirements with commercial objectives has been instrumental in supporting global expansion initiatives.

Brad is recognized for fostering high-performing teams, strengthening cross-functional collaboration, and maintaining a strong culture of compliance, safety, and accountability. His leadership approach emphasizes operational excellence, scalability, and long-term value creation.
